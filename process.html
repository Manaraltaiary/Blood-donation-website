Donation process

1. Register

  • Photo ID
  • Full name
  • Phone number

2. Health History Questionnaire

  • Health history
  • Recent travel
  • Immunization

3. Health Screening

  • Blood pressure
  • Temperature
  • Pulse
  • Hemoglobin (Iron)

4. Blood Draw

  • Clean arm
  • Insert sterile needle
  • Relax for about 5-8 minutes

5. Snacks

  • Recharge
  • Re-hydrate
